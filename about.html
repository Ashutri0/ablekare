Bansal Thakkar

FOUNDER & CEO

Mr. Bansal Thakkar founded ablekare during the COVID-19 pandemic's first wave in 2020. He noticed that the pandemic was having an equal negative impact on people's emotional and physical wellbeing. However, there was no decent option for people to consult about their mental health on the internet. This is how, in the midst of a pandemic, ablekare was born, with a mission to bring therapy to every corner of India at the press of a button.
